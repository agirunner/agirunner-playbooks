# Research Review Rubric

## Purpose
Review a research artifact for claim support, contradiction handling,
source quality, and overclaim risk.

## Use When
- a research packet, synthesis, or report is ready for review
- the workflow needs a request-changes or acceptable disposition
- conclusions may be stronger than the available evidence

## Do
- Check that the brief, scope, and conclusion still line up.
- Verify that important claims have visible source support.
- Flag contradictions, stale evidence, and unsupported inference leaps.
- Distinguish blocking evidence problems from non-blocking editorial issues.
- Call out when the honest answer should stay bounded or conditional.
- Recommend acceptance only when the report is evidence-consistent.

## Do Not
- Approve a polished report with hidden evidence gaps.
- Ignore contradictions because the overall narrative sounds plausible.
- Treat one weak citation as support for a broad claim.
- Convert review uncertainty into fake precision.

## Output
- `review_scope`
- `blocking_findings`
- `non_blocking_callouts`
- `evidence_gaps`
- `confidence_assessment`
- `recommended_disposition`
