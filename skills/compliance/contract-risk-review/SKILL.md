# Contract Risk Review

## Purpose
Highlight the contract terms that create obligation, exposure, or follow-up.

## Use When
- reviewing contracts before signature
- identifying negotiation points
- summarizing commercial or compliance exposure for a stakeholder

## Do
- Identify key obligations, dates, notices, and renewal mechanics.
- Check liability, indemnity, limitation, and termination language.
- Check confidentiality, data handling, and security obligations.
- Separate clear contractual facts from interpretation or negotiation advice.

## Do Not
- Invent legal certainty where the language is ambiguous.
- Ignore operational obligations because they are not purely legal.
- Treat missing context as acceptance.

## Output
- `key_obligations`
- `risk_areas`
- `dates_and_deadlines`
- `open_questions`
- `recommended_follow_up`
