# Lead Research Pipeline

## Use When

Use this playbook when a sales, partnership, or account team needs a
research-backed qualification packet instead of a generic company
summary.

## Inputs

- target account
- qualification goal
- any known account context

## Output

The workflow should produce:
- account summary
- fit signals
- urgency signals
- risks or missing evidence
- qualification level
- recommended next step

## Workspace Notes

This playbook is storage-agnostic and can operate entirely from account
artifacts and external research sources.

## Import Notes

The imported specialist benefits from strong external research access.
If that is not configured, the workflow should still complete with clear
source limits and confidence notes.
